 -- Buttons -- 

Study: Student will study, causing experience and stress to increase and energy to decrease
Relax: Student will relax, causing stress to decrease (rapidly) and energy to increase (slowly) 
Sleep: Student will sleep, causing energy to increase (rapidly) and stress to decrease (slowly) 
Play: Causes time to move forward
Pause: Stops time from moving 
Grades: Displays grades from your current class, as well as final grades in previous classes
Slow: Causes time to move slowly
Medium: Cause time to move moderately fast
Fast: Causes time to move very fast
Save: Saves your game so you can load it later
Load: Load a previous save file
